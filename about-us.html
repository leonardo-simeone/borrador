About Us

Online Gaming Club is a virtual club where gamers from all around the world can gather to create a friendly and fun community to play online games together. You can be part of a team that suits your games preference and time availability. Simply tell us what games you like and what time suits you best in the Join Us section and we'll do our best to pair you up with a matching team.
